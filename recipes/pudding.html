Pudding

Description

Simple pudding recipe using just milk, eggs and sugar. This one is from FOOD HOLIC youtube channel.

Ingredients

  • 200ml of Milk
  • Two Eggs
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Put sugar in a pot and cook it on medium heat.
  2. When the sugar is dissolved, add water little by litte while it's still being cooked.
  3. Put the cooked sugar in two cups.
  4. Crack two eggs and put them in a bowl.
  5. Stir them carefully not to form bubbles.
  6. Add three tablespoons of sugar into the egg mixture.
  7. Sift the eggs through a sieve twice.
  8. Warm the milk over medium heat. The temperature should not exceed 60 degrees celsius.
  9. Pour the warm milk little by little into the eggs while stirring them.
  10. Then pour the egg-milk mixture slowly into the cooked sugar cups
  11. Close the lid of the cups with aluminium foils.
  12. Prepare a small amount of water in a pot and place a towel over the water.
  13. Then put the cups on the towel and close the pot lid and cook it on medium heat for 8 minutes.
  14. After that, take out the cups carefully with heat-proof gloves and cool them off for one hour.
  15. Remove the aluminium foil cover from the cups and cool them in a refrigerator for three hours.
  16. And finally, you can take them out from the refrigerator and scrape the inside edge of the cups with a toothpick so that you can flip it upside down to pour the solid pudding on a plate, cold, soft and ready for you to feast.
